- $$u=x-1$$

- $$\int_0^1 (u+1)\sqrt{u} d(u+1)$$

- $$\int_0^1 (u+1)\sqrt{u} du$$

- $$\int_0^1 (u^\frac{3}{2}+u^\frac{1}{2}) du$$

- $$\int_0^1 (u^\frac{3}{2})du + \int_0^1 (u^\frac{1}{2}) du$$

- $$\int (u^\frac{3}{2})du = \frac{2}{5}u^\frac{5}{2}$$

- $$\int (u^\frac{1}{2})du = \frac{2}{3}u^\frac{3}{2}$$

- 最终 = 2/5+2/3=16/15
